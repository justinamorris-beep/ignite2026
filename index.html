The Ignite the Market Competition is a performance-based challenge designed to reward proactive activities, consistent effort, and outstanding results across all real estate activities. Participants earn points each week, submit them weekly, and compete for top honors and prizes in the quest to take back top market share in 2026.

How it works

  • 1.Perform approved real estate activities throughout the week to earn points.
  • 2.Submit once per heat using the Submit tab — enter a quantity for each activity you completed. Multiple of the same activity in one heat all count.
  • 3.One submission per agent per heat. Your history loads automatically when you enter your name.
  • 4.Forgot a prior heat? You can still submit for it — just select the correct heat from the dropdown.
  • 5.Points tally continuously across all heats — watch your rank rise on the Heat Index.
  • 6.The top point earner each heat gets to drive the remote-controlled firetruck around the office!
  • 7.Monthly category awards for each month will be presented at the following Team Meeting.
  • 8.Overall top 3 grand prizes announced and awarded at the Team Meeting on August 11, 2026.

All submissions are on the honor system. Management may verify activity documentation upon request.

How points work

Each activity earns points per occurrence within the heat. If you executed 3 listing agreements in one heat, enter 3 — your points calculate automatically. Bonus source points stack on top of the base activity.
